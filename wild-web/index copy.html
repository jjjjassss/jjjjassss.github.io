Today's GPS receivers are extremely accurate, thanks to their parallel multichannel designs. Our receivers are quick to lock onto satellites when first turned on. They maintain tracking locks in dense tree cover or in urban settings with tall buildings. Certain atmospheric factors and other error sources can affect the accuracy of GPS receivers. Garmin GPS receivers are typically accurate to within 10 metre. Accuracy is even better on water because there are no obstructions to interfere with the signal.
